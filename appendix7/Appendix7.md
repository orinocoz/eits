Structural notes:

- The ten core BSI module groups (ISMS through IND) have one-to-one structural correspondence with E-ITS at the top level. Individual measure numbers and scope differ due to Estonian adaptation.
- SYS.EE covers X-tee data exchange services and eID components specific to Estonian digital infrastructure.
- APP.EE covers Estonian-specific applications and AI systems with no German counterpart.
- BSI IT-Grundschutz-Kompendium Edition 2023 contains approximately 4,900 individual requirements across these module groups. The E-ITS 2024 edition contains 1,802 measures following contextual adaptation.
